# Retail Analysis

## Overview
This project presents a data-driven exploration of retail store performance across **four regions in Kenya**, covering **ten stores** dealing in diverse product lines.  
The analysis investigates store-level and product-level performance, identifies sales trends over time, and highlights insights that could inform retail strategy and inventory management.

The work demonstrates a structured approach to retail analytics and reflects data exploration, visualization, and the ability to ask critical questions that reveal underlying business dynamics.

---

## Objectives
- Examine the performance of retail stores across different regions.
- Evaluate product-level sales trends and identify top- and under-performing items.
- Explore regional sales patterns and variations in customer demand.
- Derive insights that can guide entrepreneurs considering entry into the retail sector.

---

## Tech Stack
- **Python**
- **pandas** – Data cleaning, manipulation, and aggregation  
- **numpy** – Numerical computation  
- **matplotlib** & **seaborn** – Exploratory data visualization and trend analysis  

---

## Methodology
**Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** – Visualized key metrics such as sales by region, store, and product.  
**Feature Engineering** – Derived new attributes to better understand sales behavior over time.  
**Trend Analysis** – Identified recurring sales patterns and anomalies.  

The analysis emphasizes interpretability, enabling clear communication of insights to both technical and business audiences.
